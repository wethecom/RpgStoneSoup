sx, sy = the Surface chunk that contains the entry gate.
icx, icy = which interior chunk of the castle to paint (start at 0,0 just inside the gate; (1,0) is one chunk east, etc).
Click + drag to paint · right-click to erase · Custom chunks override the game's procedural generation at their coord. Open the game and walk to (cx,cy) to test. Painting a TELEPORTER cell prompts for destination once -- it sticks until you reset it. CASTLE GATE (cstgate): paint on a Surface chunk; stepping on it in-game warps the player into the Castle pocket-branch keyed by THIS chunk's (cx,cy). Inside the castle, paint an EXIT GATE (exitgate) to send the player back to the surface cell the entry gate occupied. Dropping an NPC opens a dialog editor; click the NPC again with the Entity tool to re-edit its speech. Ctrl+Z undo · Ctrl+Y redo.
