How to play

Pick a species and a background above, then enter the dungeon. Descend the five-floor Dungeon and step onto the Orb of Zot at its bottom to win. Gold > tiles are branch passages — optional detours into the Lair, Orcish Mines, Crypt, Vaults, Swamp or Shoals for extra danger and loot. Careful play wins; careless play dies.

Move & attack

  • Arrow keys — move up / down / left / right
  • h j k l — move left / down / up / right
  • y u b n — move the four diagonals
  • or the numpad (1-9)
  • Walk into a monster to attack it in melee
  • , or . — wait one turn (lets HP regen)

Act & survive

  • g — pick up the item under you
  • > — take the down stairs (stand on >)
  • q then h — quaff a potion of healing
  • q then m — quaff a potion of might
  • i — show your inventory
  • Watch the HP bar — heal before it runs out

Tip: fight monsters one at a time in corridors rather than wading into a room full of them. Grab every healing potion you see — you will need them deeper down.

How to Play

Descend the five-floor Dungeon to the Orb of Zot at its bottom. Climb out via < and the world opens onto an endless surface of biomes, villages, castles, ruins and POIs. > uses stairs / branch passages / building entrances.

Move, fight, rest

  • Arrows / hjkl yubn / numpad — move & attack
  • , . Space — wait one turn
  • s — camp: rest until full HP+MP or interrupted
  • g — pick up item underfoot
  • > — descend stair / enter building or branch
  • < — ascend stair / exit a branch
  • Click a far tile to auto-walk; click a monster to chase it

Act & cast

  • q+h/m — quaff healing / might
  • r+t/f — read teleport / fear
  • z / v / f — cast / wand / throw
  • p pray at altar · a invoke god
  • i — inventory (click items to use)

Surface life

  • Bump a @ — talk to NPC, accept quest, or open shop
  • First bump on a guard warns; second bump attacks
  • Opening a guarded chest turns nearby guards hostile
  • Q — quest log · M — world map
  • Food bar ticks down — eat from your pack to top up
  • Cellar stairs in buildings descend into mirror sub-floors
  • Click on the gold "Editor" link on the title to build your own chunks

Tip: explore the world map (M) often. Yellow ★ marks castles with a king who hands out major quests; orange pips mark shops & manors; cyan pips mark wells. Light a beacon POI to unfurl a 5×5 area on the map without walking it.
