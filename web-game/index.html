How to play

Pick a species and a background above, then enter the dungeon. Descend the five-floor Dungeon and step onto the Orb of Zot at its bottom to win. Gold > tiles are branch passages — optional detours into the Lair, Orcish Mines, Crypt, Vaults, Swamp or Shoals for extra danger and loot. Careful play wins; careless play dies.

Move & attack

  • Arrow keys — move up / down / left / right
  • h j k l — move left / down / up / right
  • y u b n — move the four diagonals
  • or the numpad (1-9)
  • Walk into a monster to attack it in melee
  • , or . — wait one turn (lets HP regen)

Act & survive

  • g — pick up the item under you
  • > — take the down stairs (stand on >)
  • q then h — quaff a potion of healing
  • q then m — quaff a potion of might
  • i — show your inventory
  • Watch the HP bar — heal before it runs out

Tip: fight monsters one at a time in corridors rather than wading into a room full of them. Grab every healing potion you see — you will need them deeper down.

How to Play

Descend the five-floor Dungeon to the Orb of Zot at its bottom. Gold > tiles are optional branch passages (Lair, Orcish Mines, Crypt, Vaults, Swamp, Shoals); < climbs back up.

Move & attack

  • Arrow keys — move up / down / left / right
  • h j k l — move left / down / up / right
  • y u b n — move the four diagonals
  • or the numpad (1-9)
  • Walk into a monster to attack it in melee
  • , or . — wait one turn (HP slowly regens)
  • s — make camp: rest until fully healed (or enemies appear)

Act & survive

  • g — pick up the item under you
  • > — descend stairs / enter a branch passage
  • < — climb back up the stairs
  • q then h / m — quaff healing / might
  • r then t / f — read teleport / fear
  • z / v / f — cast / evoke wand / throw
  • p at an altar — pray; a — invoke a god's ability
  • i — open the inventory; a letter in it equips / quaffs / reads that item
  • ? — open / close this help

Tip: stop on each level to watch the sidebar — the Here: line names what you stand on, and a coloured row warns when you're Poisoned, Slowed or Hasted. Spot the orange ^ — that's a known trap. A healing potion also cures poison.
